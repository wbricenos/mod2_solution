Our menu

Chicken

Experience tells us that the better birds are cared for, the better the quality and flavour of the chicken they produce. All of our chicken comes from a group of carefully selected British farmers.

Beef

Beef is graded in two ways: quality grades for tenderness, juiciness and flavor; and yield grades for the amount of usable lean meat on the carcass.

Sushi

The origins of sushi lie in the way seafood used to be preserved. Seafood was fermented in Asia by adding rice. This rice was not discarded in Japan, but was instead eaten with the seafood.
